### Key Discoveries

1. **Oscar is alive** — 33 files, 5-layer GUI automation engine, fully wired via ComputerTool → tool registry. Was suspected dead; is production-active.

2. **Zero instinct definitions** — The instinct engine (V2, 0ms heuristic, LRU cache) runs on every message and returns nothing. Highest-leverage single fix: create 6 `.md` files.

3. **GatewayContext already injects everything** — `socraticEngine`, `questManager`, `groundState`, `timelineManager` are all instantiated and passed to context. Wiring cost is in the handler, not the bootstrapper.

4. **Context pipeline and instinct evaluator are architecturally isolated** — 30+ pipeline layers produce rich behavioral signals that the instinct engine never sees. This is the single biggest structural gap on the platform.

5. **A2A replaced ACP** — ACP (Agent Communication Protocol, ~600 LoC) was over-engineered and never used in production. A2A (`src/a2a/`, ~262 LoC, 4 files) is its replacement: pure transport, one concern per file, `A2ARegistry` constructor-injected into `GatewayCore`, `send()` never throws, Parliament uses `broadcast()`. See `flows/a2a-message.md`.

6. **Evolution runs on a timer, not on conversations** — Only ReflexionEngine ties to real interactions; the remaining 15 evolution files run on a 5-minute background tick regardless of whether a conversation happened.
